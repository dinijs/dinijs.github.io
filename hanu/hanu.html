Hanu Kebab is a fictional restaurant that is loosely based on a place I used to go while studying in university. The research is structured as a UI case study employing some elements from design thinking methodology.

Restaurant Concept

Restaurant Name

Hanu Kebab

Food & drinks

Kebabs with customisation:

  1. Bread: Pita / Lavash
  2. Meat: Beef / Chicken / Falafel
  3. Sauces: Yoghurt / Curry / Spicy / Sweet
  4. Onions: with / without
  5. Drinks: Black tea / Water / Coffee

Location

Berlin, Germany (Urban)

Delivery radius: City centre area

Main target audience

Busy working people

Cost

Moderate. It should be around 9€

Elevator pitch

We provide you with a tasty and nutritious lunch in a form of kebab. It will make you full and get you through a busy day!

Target Audience

The website/app will focus on the following target audiences:

Roles

  • People with limited time to have lunch during the working day
  • Health conscious people
  • People who take food from home and eat it in their office
  • Students during intensive study sessions

Demographics

  • Gender

    - both male and female

  • Marital status

    - Mostly single

  • Education

    - College students and employees with higher education

  • Occupations

    - Office work / student

  • Age

    - 19 to 45

  • Location

    - Urban

Psychographics

  • Personality & Attitudes

    - Industrious, Health conscious, Practical, Curious

  • Values

    - Open minded

  • Lifestyles

    - Active

Stakeholder needs

User Needs

The website needs to enable the user to:

  • Find out if the restaurant delivers to their area
  • Order food online
  • Customise order
  • See how long will it take for the food to be delivered
  • Receive the details of the order in the email
  • If order is made many hours in advance, be able to set a time when the food should be delivered

Client Needs

The website needs to enable the client to:

  • To sell food online that will be delivered
  • Provide a system for order customisation
  • Provide a way to pay or the order via credit card or PayPal
  • Convey the tastiness through pictures or videos
  • Communicate nutrition value of food
  • Appear functional and minimalistic

Outline of Scope

Content Requirements

  • Food menu prices
  • Pictures of different kebabs
  • Infographics about nutrition value of each kebab
  • Information about the delivery areas
  • Contact us section

Functionality Requirements

  • Ability to customise order
  • Add multiple kebabs and extras to the cart
  • Input address and contact details
  • Show delivery time and option to schedule a later delivery
  • Make payment through a 3rd party service
